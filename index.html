    About Us

    WazoMoni a leading financial service provider established in Nigeria on 1st October 2019. It seeks to bridge the gap between the economically active small and medium scale entrepreneurs seeking to grow their business by providing soft credit facilities to meet their urgent business needs. Wazo is technologically(Fintech) driven with state of the art facilities, delivery speed is equally fast with a turn around time of less than 30min at maximum. Wazomoni corporate Headoffice is located at 22, Oladun street, Idimu powerline Bus-stop. Lagos with branches strategically located across major markets in Lagos states respectively with ongoing plans to establish her presence in entire Western Nigeria states in the first phase. We pride our robust human resources management system that aids us in recruiting the best hands, experienced, and equipped with relevant tools and skills that have ensured our continuous growth in the financial sector.

    Our Mission

Our business is to promote human dignity through the development of self reliant, participatory financial institutions.
